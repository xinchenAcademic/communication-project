STUDENT GUIDE

Starting college is exciting—but it can also feel overwhelming. Don’t worry—this Survival Guide is your go-to resource for navigating Red River College Polytechnic (RRC Polytech) like a pro. Packed with must-know tips, campus secrets, and direct links to support services, it helps you study smarter, stay balanced, and make the most of your college experience.

Indigenous Supports

Welcome to RRC’s Indigenous Supports Service! This team provides the tools and resources to help Indigenous students succeed, from community to classroom to career.

We provide holistic and interconnected wrap around supports that take the best of western and Indigenous ways of teaching and learning.

We offer services like:

Advising and enrollment
Student counselling
Student employment services

Contact us through indigenous.support@academic.rrc for inquiries and questions.

This service is related to the communication strategy of “questioning” and “repairing” as it includes sharing information in counselling after which best service is provided to repair the needed things.

Library Services

Welcome to RRC’s library Service! The Library has over 50,000 titles including books, journals, reports, and government publications in print format, plus many more in digital format.

Students can borrow from a collection of over 5,000 video and DVD titles; and nearly 2,000 items of equipment including laptops, TVs, VCRs, DVD players, projectors, visual presenters, and digital cameras.

We also provide access to sevices
Research and Learning Support
Study Spaces and Technology
Book a tutor
Workshops

Contact us through library.services@academic.rrc for inquiries and questions.

This service is related to the communication strategy of “investigating” and “reflecting” as students can use library material to search for information they need and reflect upon their ides in group discussions during worshops.

Academic Success

Welcome to RRC’s Academic Success Center! This supports students in becoming independent and confident life-long learners throughout their education. We serve valuable and significant deals for students to take advantage of for free!

Academic Success Center offers different variety of supports for study strategies like:

Group Tutoring
Academic Coaching and Training
Writing Supports

Personalized assistance is also available offering features like, individual tutoring and review workshops for students to try!

Contact us through academic.success@academic.rrc for inquiries and questions

Student Employment

Welcome to RRC’s Student Employment Service! This provides students with the opportunity to gain work experience while pursuing their education.

Students have the opportunity to work on-campus or off-campus in roles that align with their field of study. It may be available through

Work-study Programs
Internships
Direct hiring by employers
We also offer in-person bookings for
  • career planning and guidance
  • job search support
  • networking

To gain benefits from this service, please approach us at student.employmen@academic.rrc for inquiries and questions

This service is also related to the communication strategy of “planning” and “participating” as it gives a step-by-step opportunity for students and the engagement they are receiving from the benefits they chose.

Diversity on Campus

Welcome to RRC’s Diversity on Campus Service! We are dedicated to provide a barrier free environment for individuals to succeed in their academic, employment and research goals.

Our goal is to provide a respectful atmosphere that is diverse, inclusive and equitable to our students, staff and external partners. We provide:

Gender-Neutral Washrooms
Reflection Rooms
The Spectrum

To gain benefits from this service, please approach us at diversityoncampus.services@academic.rrc for inquiries and questions.

This service is related to the communication strategy of “advocating” and “participating” as it helps students to speak up for themselves and those around and gives them equal chance to take part without discrimination.
